# ContentOps AI — Video Pitch (60–75s)

## Target

Audience: marketing + growth teams, founders, agencies  
Format: 16:9 (YouTube/landing) or 9:16 (Reels/Shorts)  
Tone: confident, crisp, product-forward (no hype)  

## Hook (0–6s)

Voiceover: “Marketing teams lose hours every week juggling research, briefs, drafts, approvals, scheduling, publishing, leads, and ROI reporting. ContentOps AI turns that chaos into one workflow.”
On-screen text: “Stop juggling 10 tools.”

## Storyboard (scene-by-scene)

### Scene 1 — The chaos (0–5s)

Visuals: rapid cuts of tabs, docs, chat, spreadsheets, calendar, analytics dashboards  
On-screen text: “Research → Strategy → Content → Approvals → Publish → Leads → ROI”
Voiceover: “Your campaign workflow is scattered across tools—and that’s where time and momentum disappear.”

PixVerse prompt: “Fast-paced montage of a marketer switching between browser tabs: competitor research, Google doc brief, social post drafts, approval comments, scheduling calendar, publishing dashboard, lead list, ROI chart. Modern office, cinematic lighting, smooth motion, professional corporate style.”

### Scene 2 — The command center (5–10s)

Visuals: clean dashboard UI on a laptop, calm camera move  
On-screen text: “One command center”
Voiceover: “ContentOps AI is a visual command center that runs the whole pipeline end-to-end.”

PixVerse prompt: “Cinematic close-up of a laptop showing a modern dark-mode dashboard with cards and workflow stages. Smooth camera push-in, premium SaaS aesthetic, soft depth of field, professional lighting.”

### Scene 3 — Market research agent (10–18s)

Visuals: cards labeled competitors, pain points, trends, keywords, opportunity score  
On-screen text: “Research Agent”
Voiceover: “Start with market research: competitors, pain points, trends, and the best angles for your offer.”

PixVerse prompt: “Clean animated dashboard-style visuals showing competitor list, trend cards, keyword table, and an opportunity score gauge. Modern UI, dark theme, subtle motion graphics, professional.”

### Scene 4 — Topic strategy (18–26s)

Visuals: pillar map, intent scoring, topic cards  
On-screen text: “Topic Strategy • Intent scoring”
Voiceover: “Then Topic Strategy: intent-scored ideas mapped to pillars—so every post has a purpose.”

PixVerse prompt: “Modern SaaS UI showing content pillars with topic cards and intent scores (informational/commercial/transactional). Smooth transitions, dark mode, premium look.”

### Scene 5 — Content + video studio (26–38s)

Visuals: platform drafts (LinkedIn, Instagram, X), then short video script scene list  
On-screen text: “Platform-native drafts”
Voiceover: “Generate platform-native drafts and short-form video scripts—ready for review, not rewriting.”

PixVerse prompt: “Split-screen of social media drafts for LinkedIn/Instagram/X and a short-form video script timeline with scenes and on-screen text. Clean UI, crisp typography, professional motion.”

### Scene 6 — Approval + calendar (38–50s)

Visuals: Kanban approval board, scheduled posts calendar  
On-screen text: “Approval gates • Calendar”
Voiceover: “Move through approvals with clear gates, then schedule content on a calendar optimized for consistency.”

PixVerse prompt: “Modern approval kanban board transitioning into a content calendar view with scheduled posts. Dark mode UI, smooth camera pan, clean corporate style.”

### Scene 7 — Publishing + leads + outreach (50–64s)

Visuals: publish log success states, lead list with scores, outreach message draft  
On-screen text: “Publishing • Leads • Outreach”
Voiceover: “Publish across platforms, find leads, and generate personalized outreach messages—without losing the thread.”

PixVerse prompt: “Dashboard sequence: publishing log with success badges, lead table with lead scores, and a personalized outreach message composer. Premium SaaS UI, smooth motion graphics.”

### Scene 8 — ROI analytics + CTA (64–75s)

Visuals: ROI charts, hours saved, cost saved, campaign speed  
On-screen text: “Prove ROI • Save hours”
Voiceover: “And finally: ROI analytics—hours saved, cost saved, and performance—so your team can prove impact and move faster. ContentOps AI: one workflow from campaign goal to ROI.”

PixVerse prompt: “Elegant ROI analytics dashboard with charts labeled hours saved, cost saved, posts generated, leads found. Cinematic motion, dark theme, premium SaaS aesthetic. End on a clean logo/title card: ContentOps AI.”

## Editor notes

- Pace: energetic first 10s, then steady clarity, strong finish
- Music: modern tech beat, low-mid intensity
- Captions: keep to 4–7 words per beat
- Deliverables: 8 clips (5–10s each) + voiceover track + captions overlay
